Brydee & Co is a dedicated gluten-free bakery based in Berwick, Victoria (3806). We are an online, delivery-based bakery, so the best way to reach us is by email or Instagram and we will get straight back to you.

Where we deliver

We deliver within 30km of Berwick, covering much of south-east Melbourne, including Berwick, Beaconsfield, Officer, Narre Warren, Clyde, Cranbourne, Hallam, Hampton Park, Endeavour Hills, Pakenham and Dandenong. Pickup in Berwick is also available. Full details on our delivery page.

Orders, gift boxes & wholesale

Order cookies any time through our online shop. For gift boxes and hampers, custom mixes, or wholesale and cafe stockist enquiries, email us with the details and your delivery suburb and we will send a quote.

Frequently asked

How quickly will you reply?
We usually reply within a day. For urgent orders, a message on Instagram is often fastest.
Do you take phone orders?
We are an online bakery and take orders through the website and by email, which keeps everything clear and correct.
Can I pick up instead of delivery?
Yes, pickup in Berwick is available. Let us know at checkout or by email.
