What is inside

Every gift box is filled with our handmade gluten-free, dairy-free cookies and biscuits, baked fresh in our dedicated gluten-free kitchen. We can tailor the mix and add a personal note. Great for birthdays, thank-yous, get-well gifts, corporate hampers and Christmas.

  • 100% gluten-free and coeliac-friendly, dairy-free throughout
  • No preservatives, low sugar, handmade in Berwick
  • Custom mixes and gift notes available
  • Egg-free and vegan options on request

How to order a gift box

Gift boxes and hampers are made on request. Email hello@brydeeco.com.au or message us on Instagram with what you have in mind and your delivery suburb, and we will put together a quote. We deliver across Melbourne.

Can you make a hamper for a coeliac?
Yes, that is exactly what these are for. Everything is 100% gluten-free and baked in a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, so it is safe for coeliacs.
Can I add a gift message?
Yes, just let us know what you would like the note to say when you order.
Do you do corporate or bulk gift orders?
Yes, get in touch at hello@brydeeco.com.au with the quantity and timing and we will sort a quote.
