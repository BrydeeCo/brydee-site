Brydee & Co (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy and handles your personal information in line with the Australian Privacy Principles. This policy explains what we collect and why.

What we collect

When you place an order we collect your name, email, delivery address and phone number so we can bake and deliver your order and keep you updated about it. If you email or message us, we keep that correspondence so we can help you.

Payments

Card payments are processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card details; Stripe handles that under their own security standards.

How we use your information

We use your details only to process and deliver your order, respond to enquiries, and, if you have opted in, to occasionally let you know about new bakes. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Your choices

You can ask us what information we hold about you, correct it, or ask us to delete it, at any time. Just email hello@brydeeco.com.au.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email hello@brydeeco.com.au.
