Gluten-free and dairy-free, no compromise

Every batch is baked by hand in our dedicated gluten-free kitchen in Berwick, so they are safe for coeliacs, and they are dairy-free and lactose-free too. We keep the sugar low and skip the preservatives.

  • Gluten-free and wheat-free, dedicated gluten-free kitchen
  • Dairy-free chocolate, lactose-free throughout
  • Low sugar, no preservatives, small batches

Frequently asked

Are the chocolate chips really dairy-free?
Yes, we use dairy-free chocolate, so the whole cookie is dairy-free and lactose-free as well as gluten-free.
Are they safe for coeliacs?
Yes. They are 100% gluten-free and baked in a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, so there is no cross-contamination.
Can I mix flavours in a box?
Absolutely. Order the Mixed Gluten-Free Cookie Box, or message us for a custom mix.
