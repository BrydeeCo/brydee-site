Delivery

We deliver within 30km of Berwick, which covers much of south-east Melbourne. Delivery is a flat $10, and free on orders of $100 or more. You will choose delivery at checkout and enter your address.

Pickup in Berwick

Prefer to collect? Pickup in Berwick is available at no charge. Let us know at checkout or email hello@brydeeco.com.au and we will arrange a time.

Outside our delivery area?

If you are beyond 30km or would like a larger or gift order sent further, get in touch and we will do our best to arrange it.

Freshness

Everything is baked fresh to order in small batches, with no preservatives. Your cookies are best enjoyed within a few days, and they freeze beautifully if you would like to keep them longer.

Returns & issues

Because our products are freshly baked food, we are unable to accept change-of-mind returns. Your happiness matters to us, though, so if anything is wrong with your order, or it arrives damaged, please email us at hello@brydeeco.com.au within 48 hours with a photo and we will make it right, whether that is a replacement or a refund.

Frequently asked

How much is delivery?
A flat $10 within 30km of Berwick, and free when your order is $100 or more.
Can I get a refund?
As freshly baked food, our items are not eligible for change-of-mind returns, but if there is a problem with your order, contact us within 48 hours and we will replace it or refund you.
Do you deliver Australia-wide?
Our standard delivery is local to south-east Melbourne. For orders further afield, email us and we will see what we can arrange.
