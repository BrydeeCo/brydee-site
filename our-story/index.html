Our Story

Brydee & Co started with one person who could not find a gluten-free treat worth eating, so she baked her own.

Shop the cookie jar
Home › Our Story

Brydee & Co began with a personal challenge. After discovering she had intolerances to both gluten and dairy, Brydee found herself constantly disappointed by the treats available: dry, flavourless, or packed with fillers and preservatives. If you are gluten-free or dairy-free yourself, you know exactly the feeling.

Determined to prove that allergy-friendly baking could be just as good as the real thing, she started experimenting in her home kitchen in Berwick. What began as a way to make treats she could actually enjoy quickly became something friends and family could not stop asking for.

A dedicated gluten-free kitchen

As demand grew, so did the commitment. Everything is now baked in a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, which means there is no shared equipment and no cross-contamination, so our bakes are genuinely coeliac-friendly, not just ‘made without gluten’. For anyone with coeliac disease, that distinction is everything, and it is why we take it seriously.

Made the way we feed our own kids

Brydee’s four kids are the toughest critics in Berwick and the most loyal fans. Every new batch has to pass their taste-test before it reaches you. We bake with real, recognisable ingredients, keep the sugar low, and never use preservatives, because these are the cookies our own family reaches for.

What we stand for

  • Safe: dedicated gluten-free kitchen, dairy-free throughout, coeliac-friendly
  • Honest: real ingredients, clear about what is in every bake, egg and honey included where relevant
  • Fresh: baked to order in small batches in Berwick, never mass-produced

Whether you call them cookies or biscuits, every one is made with the same care Brydee put into that very first batch. Thanks for being part of the story. — Brydee 🤍

Frequently asked

Are you really coeliac-safe?
Yes. Everything is baked in a dedicated gluten-free kitchen with no shared equipment, so there is no cross-contamination. That is what makes us coeliac-friendly rather than just gluten-free by recipe.
Where are you based?
We are a home-based bakery in Berwick, Victoria, and we deliver across south-east Melbourne.
