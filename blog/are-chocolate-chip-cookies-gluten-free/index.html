Are Chocolate Chip Cookies Gluten-Free?

Short answer: standard ones are not. Here is why, and how to get ones that are.

Home › Blog › Are Chocolate Chip Cookies Gluten-Free?

By Brydee, founder & baker · 2026-07-03

No, most chocolate chip cookies are not gluten-free. The classic recipe is built on wheat flour, which contains gluten, so a normal choc chip cookie is off-limits if you are coeliac or gluten intolerant. There are two other things to watch for too: the chocolate chips themselves can sometimes contain gluten or dairy, and even a cookie made with gluten-free flour is not safe for coeliacs if it was baked in a kitchen that also handles wheat.

Why the standard recipe contains gluten

Gluten is the protein in wheat, barley and rye that gives baked goods their structure and chew. Plain and self-raising flour are wheat-based, so unless a recipe specifically swaps in a gluten-free flour blend, the cookie contains gluten.

Can chocolate chip cookies be made gluten-free?

Absolutely, and done well they taste just like the original. A good gluten-free choc chip cookie uses a quality gluten-free flour blend and dairy-free chocolate, and it holds together and stays soft rather than turning to sand. The trick most home bakers miss is balancing the blend so you do not get that gritty, crumbly texture gluten-free baking is unfairly known for.

How to know a gluten-free cookie is coeliac-safe

This is the part that matters most if you have coeliac disease. A cookie can be made from gluten-free ingredients and still be unsafe if it shares an oven, bench or utensils with wheat flour, because tiny amounts of cross-contamination are enough to cause a reaction. The gold standard is a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, where no gluten is used at all.

That is exactly how we bake. Our gluten-free chocolate chip cookies are made in a dedicated gluten-free kitchen with dairy-free chocolate, so they are coeliac-friendly and dairy-free, and they taste like the cookie you remember, not a compromise.

Frequently asked

Are chocolate chip cookies gluten-free?
Standard ones are not, because they are made with wheat flour. You can buy or make gluten-free versions with a gluten-free flour blend.
Are the chocolate chips gluten-free?
Not always. Some chips contain gluten or dairy, so check the label, or choose cookies where the baker has already confirmed it, like ours.
Are your gluten-free choc chip cookies safe for coeliacs?
Yes. They are baked in a dedicated gluten-free kitchen with no cross-contamination, and they are dairy-free too.
