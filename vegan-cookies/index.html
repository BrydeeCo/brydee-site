Vegan and gluten-free, together

Because everything we bake is already gluten-free and dairy-free, going vegan is a simple swap for us, and we do it without the long ingredient lists and preservatives you see on most vegan biscuits. Real ingredients, gently sweetened, baked fresh.

  • Egg-free and fully vegan on request
  • Always gluten-free, dairy-free and lactose-free
  • Low sugar, no preservatives, handmade in Berwick

How to order vegan

Add a box to your cart and let us know you would like it vegan (or email hello@brydeeco.com.au before ordering). Boxes are 10 cookies for $40, delivered across Melbourne, flat $10 and free over $100.

Are your standard cookies vegan?
Our standard cookies are dairy-free and lactose-free but contain egg, and the Peanut Butter & Honey contains honey. We bake egg-free, fully vegan versions on request.
Are the vegan cookies gluten-free too?
Yes. Everything we make is gluten-free and baked in a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, vegan versions included.
How do I request vegan?
Add your box to the cart and note it in your order, or email us first at hello@brydeeco.com.au and we will sort it out.
