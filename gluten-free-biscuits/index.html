What makes our gluten-free biscuits different

Most gluten-free biscuits on a supermarket shelf are dry, crumbly and full of fillers and preservatives to make them last. Ours are the opposite: baked fresh to order, gently sweetened so they are lower in sugar, and made with real, recognisable ingredients and nothing artificial.

  • 100% gluten-free and wheat-free, dedicated gluten-free kitchen
  • Dairy-free and lactose-free
  • No preservatives and low sugar
  • Egg-free and fully vegan versions on request

Frequently asked

Are your biscuits suitable for coeliacs?
Yes. Every biscuit is 100% gluten-free and wheat-free, and everything is baked in a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, so they are coeliac-friendly.
Are the biscuits dairy-free too?
Yes, every biscuit we bake is dairy-free and lactose-free. Egg-free and fully vegan versions are available on request.
Where do you deliver?
We deliver within 30km of Berwick, covering much of south-east Melbourne, and you can pick up in Berwick. Flat $10 delivery, free over $100. See our delivery page.
How long do they stay fresh?
With no preservatives, our biscuits are best within a few days of baking, or freeze well if you want to keep them longer.
